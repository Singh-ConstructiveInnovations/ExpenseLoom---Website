Privacy Policies

01. Information Collection

We collect specific financial data including income sources, expense categories, and transaction dates to provide accurate budgeting insights. This include manual entries. We collect personal information such as Name, Email address, and Mobile number for profile management.

This data is securely stored using Firebase Firestore, ensuring encryption in transit for security. Our app uses AdMob and Firebase Analytics which may access your Android Advertising ID to provide personalized ads and track performance, while Data Retention is managed according to service provider policies.

02. Data Usage

Your data is used solely to generate personal financial analytics. We use aggregated, anonymized data to improve our budgeting algorithms. We do not sell your personal data. However, certain non-personal data (like device identifiers and usage statistics) are shared with service providers like Google AdMob and Firebase to provide app functionality and advertisements.

Third-party services used:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

03. Security

We implement personal security measures including face lock, fingerprint, for data at rest and TLS for data in transit. Your financial details are stored securely and are only accessible by you.

04. User Rights

You retain full ownership of your data. You have the right to request a copy of your information or request complete deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app settings.
